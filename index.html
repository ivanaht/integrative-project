HELPING OUR PLANET

Our product was made to help the planet by giving people with lower resources healthy and accessible food.

Our main objective is to accomplish the first and second sustainable goals of the UN and bacause of that we have created a nutritious bar made mostly of sweet potatoes, mixed nuts and honey.

.02 Sweet Boost

Our bar is made with sweet potato, mixed nuts, honey, oatmeal and dried berries.

The benefits of consuming our delicious product are that they help manage hunger, they are very accessible because of their low cost and they are a healthier option than most of the other bars in the market
